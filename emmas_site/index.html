About Me.

Hi! I’m Emma Belle Gralinski and I started my own business called Is That My Room?! I’ve always loved decorating, and now I help people make over their bedrooms one at a time. I usually design rooms for kids and teens (ages 3 to 18) to make them look exactly how they’ve always dreamed. But, I can also do adult rooms too for anyone 18 to infinity!

My Background.

I’ve always loved dreaming up new designs for bedrooms. It’s so much fun to take a plain room and turn it into something super cool that fits your personality.
